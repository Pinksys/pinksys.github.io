What I learned

Jack shit.

Rhetoric

There are three components to rhetoric in any medium: Exigence, Audience, and Constraints. When brainstorming, I made sure to craft arguments while keeping these three concepts in mind.

Data visualization

To further illustrate the points I made in my blog posts, I included cold, hard data. However, raw data is not easy to read and comprehend, so I used datawrapper.de to visualize data I gathered from Google Trends.

Recording & editing

Recoring a podcast and a video taught me a very valuable lesson: check your audio before recording everything. While editing, I learned a lot of shortcuts and features of my editing environments that I never new existed. I became much more proficient at editing (especially video) because of this class.
